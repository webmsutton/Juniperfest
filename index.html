JuniperFest is back for its 3rd year

Saturday the 13th June 2015!

Once again this much loved music festival will deliver a fantastic day of musical and family entertainment.

JuniperFest is all about family and friends coming together to enjoy great music, story telling and children's activities along with delicious food and drink all in a wonderfully relaxed atmosphere.

Set in delightful grounds JuniperFest showcases bright new musical talent alongside established artists. With two stages, a range of free children's activities, food stalls and bar, there's something for everyone at JuniperFest.

Tickets will be available from April 20th at last year's prices for a limited time only
