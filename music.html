Music

Acts confirmed so far include:

HEADLINING: The Midnight Rebel Band – The Midnight Rebel Band – Sweet Soul and Rock and Roll from one of the most exciting bands around. Powerful vocal harmonies and music production have cemented their reputation. As headliners at JuniperFest this year they are sure to end the day with a bang!
http://www.themidnightrebelband.com/

Genie and the Jivebombers – a fabulous 7-piece jive/ swing band playing an eclectic mix of original and classic tunes. You're going to need your dancing shoes on! http://genieandthejivebombers.co.uk/html/home.html

Komodo Krimes – they loved it and we loved them so much last year that Komodo Krimes are back. In their own words Komodo Krimes are...'the rare fusion of dance rhythms and world music, an indie band like no other. They were formed during the 5th alignment of Mars and Venus in January 2011. The band was christened Komodo Krimes – not for its literal values but its onomatopoeic impact.' We just think they rock! http://komodokrimes.bandcamp.com/

The Rock Doctors – have some rocking good fun with this excellent and long established cover band. Pop and rock music from 70's classics to the current day. http://www.rockdoctors.co.uk/

Crinkle Cuts - Funk, Ska and Reggae with innovative song writing and original cover versions, Crinkle Cuts never fail to get the crowd dancing. One reviewer commented “It's literally impossible to keep still when these guys play....Their tunes are ridiculously good.” Crinkle Cuts

Sam Brett – appearing on the main stage this year after his superb performance on the Orchard Stage last year, this wonderful acoustic singer/songwriter is not to be missed. https://www.facebook.com/sambrettmusic
