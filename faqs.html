FAQs

  • Is parking available on site? - There is no parking on site. Please refer to the 'Getting there and parking' section for details of local parking facilities.
  • Is there disabled parking available? - There will be limited disabled parking available. If you require disabled parking, please email juniperhillpta@gmail.com
  • What is included in the ticket price? - Along with the music, unlimited use of the inflatables, children's craft tent (1 item per child ticket) and the children's stories are included in the ticket price.'
  • Can I re-enter JuniperFest? - Yes you can. On initial entry, your ticket will be exchanged for a wristband. Re-entry will on be permitted with a valid, untampered with wrist band.
  • Can I pay on the day? – Yes, as long as JuniperFest is not sold out already.
  • Can I bring my own alcohol into JuniperFest? - No alcohol is to be brought into the event please. Bag searches will be carried out on entry and any alcohol will be confiscated and not returned.
  • Do I need to purchase tickets for all children? - All school age children will require a ticket which is £7.50 each. Younger children do not require a ticket.
  • Are there disabled toilet facilities available? - Yes.
  • Are there baby change facilities available? - Disposable change mats will be available from the control tent.
