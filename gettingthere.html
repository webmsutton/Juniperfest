Getting There and Parking

Driving

If at all possible please walk to JuniperFest. There is no parking on site. If you do need to drive to the event, please park as far from the school as possible. There is a car park behind Budgens which is a 10 minute walk to the school. If parking on the roads around the school, please ensure you do so with consideration for our neighbours and allowing for emergency vehicle access at all times. The school parking policy will apply to JuniperFest, details of the policy can be found at here

Event stewards will regularly patrol the roads adjacent to the school during JuniperFest. Any cars parked inconsiderately or irresponsibly will be asked to move.

Car share/ drive share:

To keep the number of vehicles to a minimum, we would request that you share a car with others attending the event. To keep as many cars off the roads around JuniperFest, there is a drive share scheme for the festival. If you live close to the school and have space(s) on your drive, please post details on the JuniperFest Facebook page. Spaces will be allocated on a first come first served basis. Facebook Page
